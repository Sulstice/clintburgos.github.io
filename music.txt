<font size="32">music</font>

I've experimented with many sounds since 2009, starting several solo projects and collaborative efforts, including a live 
rock band with some friends (Lampshade Sun) from 2010 to 2012. I currently use Ableton Live to create my music, and 
also write acoustic songs on a Martin Backpacker. I love to collaborate, so if you'd like what you hear, please contact me!

<b>Noizybrain</b>

My current musical alias. Under this name I made an acoustic EP while backpacking around Europe, writing and 
recording songs on a field recorder with a multi-track setting. I've also posted a few electronic tracks on SoundCloud.

<b>Wave One</b> 

The continuing studio project of a long standing collaboration with my buddy David Estes-Smargiassi. We recorded 
our first album as Wave One in span of about two weeks, then took a year to edit and refine the songs, covering several
sonic influences and focusing on the theme of dark psychedelic nostalgia. We've released two singles since then, 
revamped an old track, and have a handful of promising tunes in the works.

thought. I tend explore introspective and nihilist themes.
